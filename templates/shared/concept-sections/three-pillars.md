# Three Foundational Pillars

> **Purpose**: Distill the entire strategic differentiation into 3 memorable, defensible pillars. Each pillar represents a core competitive advantage that addresses customer pain points, leverages market gaps, and creates barriers to entry.

---

## Pillar Framework

**Design Principles**:
1. **Memorable**: 3-4 word names that stakeholders can recall
2. **Defensible**: Hard for competitors to replicate (moat analysis required)
3. **Evidence-Based**: ≥2 proof points per pillar with STRONG+ evidence tier
4. **Pain-Linked**: Each pillar addresses ≥1 pain point from Problem Analysis (top 5)
5. **Differentiated**: Explains why competitors can't/won't do this

**Pillar Selection Criteria**:
| Criterion | Weight | Description |
|-----------|:------:|-------------|
| Pain Point Coverage | 30% | Addresses highest-impact pain points (from Problem Analysis) |
| Competitive Moat | 25% | Time to imitation ≥12 months OR defensible through network effects/data |
| Evidence Quality | 20% | Proof points with STRONG+ evidence tier |
| Strategic Coherence | 15% | Pillars work together synergistically (not contradictory) |
| Market Timing | 10% | Leverages current market trends/technology enablers |
