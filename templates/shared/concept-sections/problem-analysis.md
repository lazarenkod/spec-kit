## Agent Prompt Guidance

When generating this section via research agents:

**market-researcher** should provide:
- Market timing signals (why NOW)
- Industry-wide statistics on pain points
- Convergence factors (economic, technological, competitive)

**persona-designer** should provide:
- Pain point rankings from JTBD research
- Frequency and severity data from persona interviews
- Emotional impact quantification

**competitive-analyst** should provide:
- Gaps in competitor solutions (what pain points they DON'T solve)
- Market white space analysis

**Synthesis Instructions**:
1. Consolidate pain points from all research agents
2. Rank by Impact Score (Severity × Frequency)
3. Group statistics into 3-4 categories
4. Write Perfect Storm narrative tying to market timing
5. Link pain points to personas and JTBDs
