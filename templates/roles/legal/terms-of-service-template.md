# Terms of Service Template

## Purpose

Generate comprehensive terms of service for web applications and SaaS products. Covers service usage, user obligations, liability limitations, and dispute resolution.

> **Disclaimer**: This template is a starting point, not legal advice. Consult a qualified attorney for your specific jurisdiction and use case.

---
