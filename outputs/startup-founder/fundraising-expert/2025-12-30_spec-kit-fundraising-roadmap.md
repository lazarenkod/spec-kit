## Executive Summary

**Spec Kit** - это open-source инструментарий для Spec-Driven Development (SDD), позволяющий малым командам создавать стартапы за недели вместо месяцев. Продукт автоматизирует полный цикл разработки: от спецификаций до работающего приложения с UI, landing pages и маркетинговыми материалами.

**Цель**: Построить компанию с $20M ARR.

**Рекомендуемый путь**: Bootstrap -> Pre-seed/YC -> Seed -> Series A

## 9. Term Sheet Priorities

### 9.1 Critical Terms to Protect

**Valuation & Economics**:
| Term | Standard | Negotiate For |
|------|----------|---------------|
| Pre-money valuation | Market rate | Fair based on comparables |
| Liquidation Preference | 1x non-participating | Never >1x, never participating |
| Anti-dilution | Broad-based weighted average | Avoid full ratchet |
| Option Pool | 10-15% pre-money | Negotiate size & timing |

**Control & Governance**:
| Term | Standard | Negotiate For |
|------|----------|---------------|
| Board composition | Founders + 1 investor | Maintain founder control |
| Protective provisions | Standard vetos | Limit scope |
| Drag-along | Majority approval | Higher threshold (60-66%) |
| Information rights | Quarterly financials | Reasonable scope |

### 9.2 2024-2025 Market Standards

Based on Cooley Q2 2025 data:
- **98% of deals**: 1x liquidation preference
- **95% of deals**: Non-participating preferred
- **90%+ of deals**: Investor veto rights (standard)
- **Standard**: Broad-based weighted average anti-dilution

### 9.3 Red Flags to Avoid

**Toxic Terms**:
- Participating preferred (double-dip)
- Multiple liquidation preferences (>1x)
- Full ratchet anti-dilution
- Cumulative dividends
- Excessive protective provisions
- Pay-to-play clauses (early stage)
- Redemption rights

**How to Push Back**:
1. Reference market standards (Cooley data)
2. Get competitive term sheets
3. Have experienced counsel (Wilson Sonsini, Cooley, Fenwick)
4. Walk away if necessary
